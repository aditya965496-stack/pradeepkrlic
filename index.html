Tax Benefits

Save Tax with LIC

LIC policies offer multi-layered tax benefits under the Income Tax Act.

📝

Section 80C — Premium Deduction

Premiums paid (up to ₹1,50,000 per year) are deductible from your taxable income. Applicable to most LIC plans including endowment, term and ULIP.

🎯

Section 10(10D) — Tax-Free Maturity

Maturity and surrender proceeds are completely tax-free, provided annual premium is ≤10% of sum assured (or ≤15% for disabled policyholders).

💸

Section 10(10A) — Commuted Pension

Commuted (lump sum) pension from LIC pension plans like Jeevan Shanti is exempt from tax for government employees and partially for others.

👨‍👩‍👧

Section 80DD / 80DDB

Premiums for policies covering a disabled dependant can attract additional deductions under these sections (up to ₹1.25 lakh).

💰

Death Claim — Fully Tax Free

Amount received by nominee on death of the life assured is completely exempt under Section 10(10D), regardless of the sum assured amount.
